# Roper's Financial Growth (Jellison Era)

- **EBITDA Margins:** Grew from 19% to 23%.
- **Free Cash Flow Margins:** Grew from 13% to 18%.
- **Comparison:** Jellison famously compared his track record against Warren Buffett's, asserting that his capital allocation per dollar was superior in certain periods.
- **Stock Performance:** Under his leadership, Roper significantly outperformed the S&P 500 and most industrial peers.
