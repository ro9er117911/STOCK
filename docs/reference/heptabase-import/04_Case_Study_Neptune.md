# Neptune: The Proof of Concept

- **Acquisition:** Neptune (Water Meters).
- **Rationale:** Jellison's first major bet at Roper.
- **Innovation:** Shifted from just selling meters to providing **Automated Meter Reading (AMR)** systems.
- **Effect:** Transformed a cyclical product seller into a stable, technology-driven recurring revenue stream. Proved to the board that his "technology-enabled" strategy worked.
