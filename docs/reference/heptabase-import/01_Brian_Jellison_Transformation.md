# Brian Jellison & Roper's Transformation

- **Role:** CEO of Roper Technologies (2001–2018).
- **Background:** 30+ years in industrial systems (GE, Ingersoll Rand).
- **Mission:** To prove that a contrarian philosophy focusing on capital allocation could transform a cyclical industrial company into a compounding powerhouse.
- **Legacy:** Transformed Roper from a manufacturing-heavy firm into an asset-light, multi-industry technology company.
